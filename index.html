01 — Philosophy

Why existing services
don't work

The problem

Spotify / Apple Music

They optimise for retention — so you stay. That means recommending what you already know. The algorithm loops: you listen to indie rock, you get more indie rock. Forever.

The problem

SoundCloud

Music is part of our daily routine — creativity that walks hand in hand with us. Sharing it with friends is a must, and SoundCloud started to realise this. But it never added the ability to share your mood. Just music, no feeling behind it.

Our approach

Discovery-first

We don't optimise for the session. We optimise for six months from now. The goal: in half a year, you're listening to music you didn't know existed today — and it feels entirely yours.

Our approach

Share your mood, not just a track

We want you to share your inspiration and feeling with friends — accompanied by a photo that captures the moment. Not just "here's a song" but "here's where I am right now."

02 — The User

Meet Alexa

Alexa
22 years old — Milan

A model. Her solo hours are filled with the gym, shoots, her personal blog, and late-night walks through Milan. Music is on, always.

Her daily flow
To keep her blog fresh and her creativity alive, Alexa needs the right music. Not random — intentional. A daily stream that fuels new ideas, new aesthetics, new energy. The right playlist is the difference between an ordinary day and one worth sharing.
Walking home through Milan at night
On the way back, Alexa likes to return inward. To reflect on the day, drift through memory, feel the gentle pull of nostalgia. For this, she needs her Home playlist — familiar, atmospheric, soft. Music that lets her breathe and look back with warmth.
Her social world
Alexa is open, popular, and socially alive. She loves sharing her vibe with others. She uses Flow to stay on the same wavelength as the people around her — music as a bridge, not just a solo experience.

02
Quiz

Your DNA session

No genre checkboxes. Smart questions: context, images, associations, mood. Short but precise. Questions transition with a veil-like animation — gentle, blue, like silk in wind.

The system learns two things: where you are and where you want to be. Then it finds the music bridge between them.

03
Playlist

Your Flow arrives

10 tracks appear from above, dropping softly into place. The playlist name is AI-generated — poetic, precise, tuned to your mood. The animation changes based on mood: clouds or rays in light theme, fog or lightning in dark.

Short transitions between tracks — like a DJ set. The music never stops abruptly. It flows.
